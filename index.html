JEFFREY DALTON

NYU Graduate11 Years of Teaching ExperienceDirector of Test Preparation Services

Jeffrey graduated from New York University with a Bachelor’s Degree in Linguistics and Languages. After College, he taught English in Lahore, Pakistan for four years where he helped middle school students develop their literacy in English. After returning to the USA, Jeffrey worked for 2 years as a Math and Verbal tutor for SAT, ACT, GRE, and GMAT in New York and New Jersey for a leading international test prep company before making the shift to Dubai. He has scored in the top 99th percentile on both the SAT and ACT. With 10 years of teaching experience, Jeffrey capably and efficiently guides students to achieve their target scores on standardized tests.

PATRICK FITZ

SAT/ACT Instructor at Hale UPENN Graduate Tested at 99th Percentile in SAT/ACT/GRE

Patrick graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a Bachelor’s degree in Economics and English Literature. While in school, he tutored primary school students in math and literacy. Following graduation, Patrick served as a Peace Corps Volunteer in Cameroon for three years, teaching English and information and Communications Technology to secondary school students. He then taught Business and English language curricula, as well as SAT, ACT, and GRE Math and Verbal at a leading education services company in Hong Kong before joining Hale Education Group. As a high school student, Patrick earned a perfect score on the ACT, scored in the 99th percentile on the SAT, and was a National AP Scholar.
